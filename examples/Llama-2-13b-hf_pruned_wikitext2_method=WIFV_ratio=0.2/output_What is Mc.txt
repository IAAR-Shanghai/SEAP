What is the history of McDonald's?
In 1940, two brothers, Ray Kroc and Richard McDonald, opened the first McDonald's restaurant in Des Plaines, Illinois. The restaurant was a success, and by 1950, there were 1,000 restaurants worldwide. In 1955, the company introduced the Big Mac, which became the most popular burger in the world. Today, McDonald's operates more than 36,000 restaurants in 119 countries around the world.
